X. Adjectives

December 9, 2019

Now, when we need describe a noun we use adjectives:

  • The boy is very big

In this sentence, the adjective is “Big”. But why?

Because this world describe a noun, in this sentences the noun is a boy. Adjectives can describe singular or plural nouns and can describe pronouns.

  • They have a big cat

They is a pronoun. (Pronoun is the same thing that a noun, we talk about this topic in another topic).

But! How can you tell wich words are adjectives?

  1. we can say that the adjective have a common endings like this:

    Al- Accidental, personal, regional.
Ful- beatiful, colorful, skillful.
Ic- historic, athletic, basic.
Ish- Selfish, foolish, pinkish.

  2. Structure of the sentences:

    subject + linking verb + adjective.
or
Articles + adjective + noun.

Linking verb could be smell, taste. For example: “This cake taste delicious”.

With adjective we have a order to use and this order is the following:

Number, opinion, size, shape, condition, age, color, pattern, origin, material, purpose.

We can remember this order with this sentence:

  • “Now on small slim couches a cat puts on magic pajamas”.

Examples

We have a big dog in the garden

The neighbours want to share their car with us. They car are big, new, red and unique

I have a big watch

I want to buy a house in the town. The house is the number 16 in the neighbour, is big an new.
