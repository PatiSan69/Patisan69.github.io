XVI. Prepositions

December 10, 2019

For the correct write in English you must know the correct use of every prepositions in English grammar and this could be a little confuse.

A preposition is a word used to link nouns, pronouns or phrases with other words in a sentences. The prepositions can change depending of the context, situation or time that you want to express.

If we want simplify this problem we can clasified in 3 parts:

Prepositions with transportation

  1. on

  2. in

We can use “on” with open spaces and platforms and in closed spaces we use “in”.

look this example:

  • *Mari is the best friend that I have and she go in motocycle to class*

  • *I had been acamping when john came to the town. He travel on bus*.

It can be a little tricky because you can say that a bus is closed spaces but es big and in English we put with “on”.

Prepositions with units of time

  1. at

  2. in

  3. on

With at we can express hours.

With in we can express weeks, months, years and decades.

Placement of prepositions in a sentence.

In this topic we can talk about prepositions phrases. Prepositions phrases are prepositions with the noun that go with them.

In a sentence we can have an adverb and adjective. like this

  • the house with the big door in my neighborhood is always open in the morning

In this example we can see that we have a noun that in this case is the house, next to him come the adjective “the big door in my neighborhood” we are describe the main subject of the sentence that is the house, then we have the verb of the sentence “is always open” and we have the adverb of the sentence “in the morning” that describe the verb in the sentence. If you can pay attention you can see that we put this sentence with a order and is true!!. This is the correct order to make a sentence in English.

So, prepositions phrases have the following order:

  1. Manner

  2. Place.

  3. Time

  • The final test in my university is tomorrow in the morning
